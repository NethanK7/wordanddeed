Our Story

Faith in Action
Since 1972

It began with a garage, thirty children from the canal banks of Colombo, and an unshakeable conviction that every life has immeasurable worth. Cynthia and Alfred Devadason saw families living in poverty along polluted waterways — and they chose to act.

Today, Word & Deed Lanka extends that same love to 400+ villages across the island, equipping communities not just to survive — but to flourish in body, mind, and spirit.
