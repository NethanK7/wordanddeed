About Word & Deed Lanka

Home / About Us

Our Mission

To empower communities across Sri Lanka through the transforming power of God's Word — meeting physical needs while nurturing spiritual growth, so that every person can live with dignity, purpose, and hope.

Our Vision

A Sri Lanka where every child, every family, and every community experiences wholeness — spiritually, physically, and socially — and where a generation raised in love goes on to win the next generation for lasting transformation.

How It Began

Born From a
Burden for the Poor

In 1972, Cynthia and Alfred Devadason walked along the canal banks of Colombo and saw what others had learned to look past — hundreds of families living in makeshift shelters, children with no school to attend, no safe place to play, and no one speaking into their future.

They opened a garage. They started a club — The Good News Club — for thirty children. That act of faithfulness sparked what would become The Lanka Vacation Bible School Movement, and eventually, Word & Deed Lanka.

Today, under the leadership of CEO Timothy Daniel, the organisation reaches 60,000+ children and 400+ villages — still driven by the same conviction that every life is immeasurably valuable.
