Your Gift At Work

What Your Gift Provides

$10

Provides a week of meals

A $10 gift feeds a child at our learning centres for one full week — ensuring they can focus and learn.

$25

Sends 5 Bibles to rural communities

A $25 gift puts the Word of God into the hands of five families — in their own language — for the very first time.

$50

Sponsors a month of education

A $50 gift covers a month of schooling for a child — tuition, materials, and a Christian values-based curriculum.

$100

Funds a community outreach event

A $100 gift resources a village outreach — bringing the Gospel and practical care to an entire community in one day.

$250

Trains a community leader

A $250 gift sponsors a leader through our discipleship programme — equipping one person to transform a community.

Our Commitment to You

100% of program-designated gifts go directly to the field — no overhead deductions.

Registered as a Social Service Organisation with the Government of Sri Lanka since 1985.

Audited annually and fully financially transparent — see our accountability report on request.

You'll receive a gift receipt and regular updates on how your support is making a difference.
