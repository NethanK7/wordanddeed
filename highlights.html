Education · June 2023

New School in Matton Opens Doors to 150 Children

In June 2023, Word & Deed Lanka inaugurated a new educational facility in Matton — a community that had long lacked access to quality schooling. The school now serves 150 children with a full Christian curriculum, trained teachers, and a daily meal programme.

"Before this school, our children had nowhere to go," said one parent from the village. "Now they wake up eager. They have hope. Word & Deed Lanka didn't just build a school — they changed the trajectory of our entire village."
